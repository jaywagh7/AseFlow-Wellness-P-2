Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Aseflow different from protein powders?

Unlike traditional protein powders that require mixing and can cause bloating, Aseflow delivers 25g of premium protein in a ready-to-drink 40ml shot. The liquid format allows for faster absorption and eliminates the need for mixing or bulky shakers.

How many shots should I consume in a day?

For most active individuals, 1–2 shots per day are ideal. Athletes or those with higher protein needs may benefit from 2–3 shots daily. We recommend consulting a nutritionist for personalized guidance based on your specific goals.

What is the shelf life of Aseflow shots?

Aseflow protein shots have a shelf life of 6 months when stored in a cool, dry place at room temperature. Once opened, consume immediately for optimal freshness and effectiveness. No refrigeration is needed before opening.

Is Aseflow suitable for vegetarians?

Yes, we offer both marine and vegan protein options. Our vegan formula is 100% plant-based, making it suitable for vegetarians and vegans. However, our marine protein is sourced from sustainably harvested fish and is not vegetarian-friendly.

When is the best time to consume Aseflow?

Aseflow can be consumed anytime you need a protein boost. Many users prefer taking it post-workout for muscle recovery, but it’s also great as a breakfast protein, mid-day snack, or before bedtime to support overnight recovery.

Do Aseflow shots contain any allergens?

Our marine protein shots contain fish-derived ingredients and should be avoided by individuals with fish allergies. Our vegan protein shots are free from common allergens such as dairy, soy, gluten, and nuts. However, we recommend reviewing the ingredient list if you have specific sensitivities.

How should I dispose of empty Aseflow shots?

Our shot containers are made from recyclable materials. Please rinse them and dispose of them in your local recycling bin. We’re committed to sustainability and continuously work to reduce our environmental footprint.

Can I use Aseflow for weight management?

Yes, Aseflow can be an effective part of a weight management plan. The high protein content promotes satiety and helps preserve lean muscle mass, supporting a healthy metabolism. Many users rely on Aseflow as a low-calorie, convenient protein source while managing their weight.
